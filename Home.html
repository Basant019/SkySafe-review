How SkySafe Works

1️⃣

Search Location

User selects a city from the Weather page. The city is stored securely using localStorage.

2️⃣

SkySafe fetches real-time and forecast weather data using trusted weather APIs.

3️⃣

Weather conditions like rain, storms, and temperature are analyzed to determine safety levels.

Users receive alerts, safety scores, and trip recommendations instantly.
